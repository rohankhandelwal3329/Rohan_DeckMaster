Game Rules

Everything you need to know before your first hand

♠ Objective

The goal of DeckMaster is to accumulate the most points by the end of the final round. Points are earned by playing cards strategically, winning tricks, and completing bonus objectives during each round.

♥ Card Hierarchy

  • Ace — Highest value card (14 points)
  • King — 13 points
  • Queen — 12 points
  • Jack — 11 points
  • 10 through 2 — Face value points

♦ Suit Priority

  • Spades ♠ — Trump suit (highest priority)
  • Hearts ♥ — Second priority
  • Diamonds ♦ — Third priority
  • Clubs ♣ — Lowest priority

♣ Turn Structure

  • Each round consists of 5 turns
  • On your turn, play one card from your hand to the play area
  • The highest-value card wins the trick (suit priority breaks ties)
  • The trick winner leads the next turn
  • After all turns, scores are tallied for the round

🏆 Winning Conditions

  • Quick Draw — Best of 5 rounds (first to 3 wins)
  • Royal Siege — Highest cumulative score after 7 rounds
  • Blitz Battle — Last player with points remaining wins

Strategy Tips

Sharpen your gameplay with these proven tactics

🎯

Control the Lead

Win early tricks with mid-range cards to dictate the pace and force opponents to reveal their high cards.

🛡️

Hold Your Aces

Save Aces and Kings for critical moments. Playing them too early wastes their power when you need it most.

♠

Trump Wisely

Spades are trump, use them to steal tricks, but don't burn them all in one round.

📊

Count Cards

Track which high-value cards have been played. This gives you an advantage in later rounds.
